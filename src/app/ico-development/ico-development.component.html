Initial Coin Offering (ICO) is a type of funding done at the initial level of a business or project. The ICO development process is primarily carried out through crowdfunding and cryptocurrencies are offered to potential investors who show their interest in funding a particular project. These cryptocurrencies are provided in the form of tokens such as utility tokens and payment tokens.

The fundraising is primarily divided into two major phases. The first phase, the Pre-sale phase, runs for a longer duration. The investors are usually offered various bonuses and discounts while purchasing the crypto coins. The primary objective of a Pre-sale ICO is to reach the soft cap as defined in the white paper, which is the minimum fund requirement for the project. The second phase is the public sale, which aims to reach the hard cap or the maximum attainable limit.
